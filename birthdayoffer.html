      NOTICE

The program is applied only for members who have been registered for our Rewards Plus Card for at least two (02) months) and need to make a cumulative purchase within 12 months from one month before the previous birth month. “A Birthday Message” will be sent to the phone number which is used to register for Rewards Plus Card before. “A Birthday Message” contains a promotion code from The AFG VN

Promotion code is required to receive any benefit if you would like to receive at mentioned brand in the SMS. Please keep the promotion code until it gets to the expiry date and show your promotion code (in SMS or email) and your Rewards Plus Card to server when you order.

The membership level is defined by your total spending at our all restaurants, at all brands within 12 months:

- To members who have registered for Rewards Plus Card over one (01) year: Calculate from one month before the previous birth month (Ex: If a customer has birthday in September 2019, Total Spending amount will be sum from 1st August 2018 to 31st July 2019)
- To members who registered for Rewards Plus Card less than one (01) year: Calculate from the date you activate the card to one month before the coming birth month. (Ex: If a customer has birthday in September 2019, Total Spending amount will be sum from the activated date of Rewards Plus Card to 31st July 2019)
- Membership levels are corresponding to the Total Spending Amount as the table below

The system will automatically double your points if you dine at the restaurant on your Birthday. Rewarded Birthday points are also added to your Rewards Plus Card account automatically. Please login your account to view your points in detail.

Free item (Pizza, Pasta, coffee, drink, ...) is  a one-time-offer for the card holder only (who received the Birthday Message) , does not apply for the total number of guests who come together with the card holder. Applies for Dine-in and take away.

The offer of free item is not applied at the same time with Point Redemption or any other promotion/discount.
