Based on true Aussie hospitality, the Al Fresco’s Group Vietnam (AFG Vietnam) serves some of the tastiest Western favourites in Vietnam.

Offering a mix of Tex-Mex, Italian, Asian-Fusion and Australian food, complemented with cold local & imported beers and an extensive wine list, featuring Australian's finest & affordable South American reds, AFG Vietnam is the best choice for a big feed at a great price. 

Considered a “godsend” by the expat community and hugely popular with Vietnamese families, couples and groups of friends, with it’s clean, comfortable and casual feel, Al Fresco’s offers a welcoming oasis in the hustle & bustle where you get great quality food, amazing customer service and great value.
